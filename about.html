Why I Built MyBizStudies

After two decades of teaching Business Studies, I kept seeing the same problem. Students were drowning in textbook content that was either too dense, too theoretical, or completely disconnected from how exam answers actually need to be structured.

I grew up helping my parents run their business and spent years helping others market theirs. That practical experience taught me that Business Studies should feel real, not like memorising a textbook.

So I built MyBizStudies. An interactive platform where graphs actually move, concepts come alive through real world examples, and students learn a proven framework for writing answers that score full marks. No fluff. No unnecessary information. Just everything you need to Ace your A-Level Business Exams.
