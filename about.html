About MyBizStudies

Built by an experienced teacher who understands what students really need to succeed.

Our Mission

MyBizStudies was created because students deserve revision resources that work the way they actually learn. Textbooks are packed with unnecessary information, static diagrams that do not show how concepts interact, and exam technique is barely covered. We built an interactive platform where students can see graphs move, learn from real world examples, and develop a framework for writing exam answers that score full marks.

Meet the Teacher

  • Over 20 years of classroom experience teaching Business Studies
  • Taught across multiple exam boards including AQA, Edexcel, CIE, and OCR
  • BCom Accounting degree and PGCE qualified
  • Unique perspective from growing up helping others market their businesses and helping parents run theirs
  • Passionate about making Business Studies accessible and practical, not just theoretical

Why We Are Different

Three key differentiators that set MyBizStudies apart:

Interactive Learning

Exam Technique Focus

No Fluff
