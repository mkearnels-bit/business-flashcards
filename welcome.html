One last thing

Just a quick heads up before you dive in

MyBizStudies is not affiliated with any exam board. All questions, mark schemes and feedback on this platform are generated using AI. While we've worked hard to align everything with the style and standards of each exam board, AI can make mistakes and we're always improving.

We make no guarantees about exam results. Your primary sources of learning should always be your exam board specification, textbook, teacher and official past papers. Think of MyBizStudies as an extra tool in your revision toolkit, not a replacement for the real thing.

There's no magic wand here. The students who get the most out of this platform are the ones who put in the work, use the feedback to improve, and combine it with their other study resources.

If you ever spot something that doesn't look right, we'd genuinely love to hear about it. Your feedback helps us make this better for everyone.
