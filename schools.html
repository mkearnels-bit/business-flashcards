What you can do as a teacher

Run AI-marked practice for your whole class.

Built by a Business Studies teacher. Every feature came from a real classroom problem.

Manage classes by year and board

Set up Year 10 AQA GCSE, Year 12 Edexcel A-Level, however your department teaches. Each class gets a join code your students use once.

Push questions and tests to a whole class at once

One generation, every student gets the same question. Standard tests follow the board's paper structure. Custom tests let you set the marks for each question yourself.

AI marking that's board-aware

The marker uses the right AO splits, command words and case study conventions for your board, so the score and feedback look like real exam-paper marking.

One markbook for the whole class

Rows are students, columns are assignments, cells are scores. Click a cell to see exactly what the student wrote and what the AI told them.

Add your own feedback when you want

Drop a personal note on any student attempt. They see it labelled "Your teacher's note" alongside the AI feedback in their portal.

Print PDFs for handwritten work

Download any question or test as a print-ready PDF. Lines per question follow the standard exam paper conventions for your level.

What your students get

A focused portal that only shows what their teacher pushes.

No noise, no distractions. Every assignment lands in their To Do list with a clear due date. Their feedback shows up the moment they submit.

  • Their own portal with assignments, scores and a record of every attempt they've completed.
  • Detailed feedback for every answer. Strengths, things to improve, and next steps. Exam-board specific.
  • AI coaching summaries generated automatically every five attempts so students see their progress and patterns over time.
  • Downloadable PDF of every completed attempt with question, answer and feedback. Useful for revision and showing parents.
